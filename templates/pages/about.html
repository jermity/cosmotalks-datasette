The data is gathered from at present [155 diverse sources](/cosmotalks/series) (called 'series') that have published talks from conferences, seminars, workshops, course lectures, summer schools, colloquia, etc. This collection was started out of personal interest because no one site was found that comprehensively compiles such data from a wide range of sources. Several categories of talks (labeled '**series**' in the data) are included, e.g.: + Cosmologists talking about their recent papers (e.g., [Cosmology Talks hosted by Shaun Hotchkiss](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCstdttIo3HM6h3hDk_v2hug/videos), [Cosmology from Home](https://www.cosmologyfromhome.com/)) + Lectures (e.g., summer schools etc, such as those offered by [ICTP](https://www.ictp.it/programmes/career-development.aspx)) + Conference talks (e.g., [ESO H0 2020](https://www.eso.org/sci/meetings/2020/H0.html), [TeVPA](https://indico.cern.ch/event/828038/), [Neutrino 2020](https://conferences.fnal.gov/nu2020/)) + Talks sponsored by academic or other institutions (e.g., [KITP](https://www.kitp.ucsb.edu/), [KIPAC](https://kipac.stanford.edu/), [NORDITA](https://www.nordita.org/video/index.php?ev=advcosmo2017), [Perimeter Institute](http://pirsa.org/), [QMUL](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCSqkcW7CMS5X7TArwO5Vpbw/videos), [Simons Foundation](https://www.simonsfoundation.org/sf-events/simons-foundation-lectures/), etc) + Collected talks by individuals (e.g., [Licia Verde](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C_nim3H7GYE&list=PLb0Uu8daWIA0eDBxZEHvf5-l2OAUCujZW), [Risa Wechsler](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL93BCCE8FAC40EBD5)) + Miscellaneous departmental seminars, workshops, colloquia, etc (e.g., [KICP Colloquia](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCejbBNzH6AfWNwg5V_AQGlg/videos))

Where a series includes some talks that are not cosmology related, those non-cosmology talks are generally not listed here.
